## Dataset Overview
The sinking of the Titanic is one of the most infamous shipwrecks in history.  
This dataset contains demographic and passenger information for **891 out of 2,224 passengers and crew**.

---

## Tools Used
- **Python** – Core logic and analysis
- **Pandas / NumPy** – Data cleaning and manipulation
- **Matplotlib / Seaborn** – Statistical data visualizations

---

## Key Insights
- **Women and Children First Policy:** Female passengers had a significantly higher survival rate than males.
- **Socio-economic Status:** Passengers traveling in **1st Class** were far more likely to survive than those in **3rd Class**.
